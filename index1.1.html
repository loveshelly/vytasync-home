How it works
1

Sensor layer reads your position

Pressure sensors embedded in the pillow identify whether you're on your side, back, or shifting between — continuously, all night.

2

AI calculates the right height

The bedside unit computes the optimal support profile for your current position. All processing is local — zero cloud, zero latency.

3

Airbags adjust in <3 seconds

Each of the three zones adjusts independently. Side-sleeping zone inflates more; back-sleeping zone deflates — silently, at ≤20 dB.

03
Breath Assist
Snore reduction · airflow optimisation
Function 03 — Breath Assist

Snoring isn't a habit.
It's your airway collapsing — and it's fixable.

Snoring is often caused by airway collapse when neck position tilts the head backward. Vytasync's breath assist function monitors your breathing rhythm via the sensor layer and — when irregular patterns are detected — gently elevates the neck to reopen the airway.

No mask. No straps. No devices on your face. Just a pillow that responds to how you breathe.
