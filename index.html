Questions, answered.

Is my sleep data private? +
All processing runs locally inside the Vytasync Box. No data ever leaves your home.
How loud is it at night? +
20dB — quieter than a library whisper. Imperceptible to most sleepers.
What if I don't love it? +
30-night trial, full refund, no questions. Free return shipping worldwide.
Does it work for all sleep positions? +
Yes — side, back, and combination sleepers. The system detects your position and adjusts each zone accordingly.
Soft-Flex vs. Hard-Solid: Which one is right for me? +
Choose Soft-Flex if you prioritize all-day comfort and adaptive movement. Choose Hard-Solid if you need high-performance stability and a more structured response.
Can it help with snoring? +
Consistent cervical alignment helps maintain a naturally open airway during sleep — reducing the micro-collapses responsible for snoring and shallow breathing. The breathwork mode also guides your nervous system into a calmer state before and during sleep. Note: Vytasync is not a medical device and results vary by individual.
